Importance of responsive design and its impact on the target audience.

The importance of responsive design ensures that your content adapts to various sizes and resolutions to provide the optimum experience for users.

Describe three different kinds of reports that will tell you what information your target audience is accessing.

The first kind of report that will tell you what information your target audience is accessing is the Enhanced Ecommerce feature in Google's analytics. This feature allows you to find which products are the most popular by tracking the most button clicks.

The second report is The Goal Flow report, which helps improve user experience by showing users who have completed a purchase which is considered a goal.

The third report would be Analytic Intelligence, which is a feature used to ask questions and getting a fast response. This feature allows Analytic Intelligence to discover whats important in your data and stores the information.
