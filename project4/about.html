About Catering


Food for the Soul

Food for the Soul Catering Company was founded by Christopher Jacobson, a young chef in his 30's that began with a food truck and is now venturing out to the catering world.He graduated from Johnson and Wales Culinary School and uses his skills learned and encorporates his knowledge into his dishes. We only use seasonal ingredients.

Food for the Soul Catering company specializes in cajun southern cuisine as well as caribbean inspired dihes.
